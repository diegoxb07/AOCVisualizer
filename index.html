Help & Feature Guide

Full documentation and source: github.com/diegoxb07/AOCVisualizer · Built for the NOAA Aircraft Operations Center · Science Branch.

The Mission Visualizer replays NOAA hurricane-hunter flight-level data together with synced MMR video, a live map tracker, a Primary Flight Display, fully customizable graphs, satellite and storm-track overlays, and video export. It runs entirely in your browser, and the recon API is optional. This guide breaks down every feature.

Keyboard shortcuts

  • Space plays or pauses.
  • Left / Right arrows scrub the timeline (hold to accelerate).
  • Shift + Left / Right jumps 10 flight-minutes.

1. Loading a mission

Both paths feed the same parser, so the map, charts, PFD, and export behave identically.

  • Archive browser (needs API online): in the top-left card, type in the search box to find a mission by id, storm name, or date (YY-MM-DD), or pick Year, Storm, and Flight from the dropdowns, then click Load Flight + Storm Track. This streams the full-resolution mission NetCDF plus the storm's whole-life best-track.
  • Manual upload (always works, no internet): drop a .nc file (for example 20221028H1_A.nc) on the "or upload" zone. When the API is offline the archive greys out with an "API Offline" banner and the upload button enlarges. It re-checks every 60 seconds and re-enables itself.
  • Pre-load Flight Data downloads and parses missions in the background. They save on this device and reopen instantly from Previously Loaded Missions, even after a reload.
  • Metrics Across Flights finds which loaded flight recorded the highest or lowest value of any metric, with a comparison graph (works offline).

2. Playback and time window

Everything lives in the sticky bottom bar. Set the Flight-Data Start and End times (HHMMSS UTC) to replay just a segment, and the map, charts, PFD, and timeline render only that window.

  • Play and Pause, playback speed, and Reset (jump to the window start).
  • Timeline slider scrubs anywhere, and the UTC readout updates live.
  • 8Hz Smoothing (map header) interpolates between the 1-second samples for fluid motion, recommended for training.
  • With a synced MMR video loaded, the video clock drives playback. Otherwise the engine advances on its own clock.

3. Map tracker (2D and 3D)

Switch with the 2D Map Tracker / 3D Tracker dropdown in the map header.

  • 2D: a whole-world canvas map (coastlines, US states) with satellite imagery and wind barbs. Wheel to zoom and drag to pan, and a Recenter on Aircraft button reappears once you pan away.
  • 3D: a Three.js scene with terrain and a plane model, the track drawn by GPS or pressure altitude. Orbit and zoom with the mouse.
  • Bottom-bar options: Track Color (wind speed or warming/cooling), Barb Color (wind speed or hurricane wind field, which stays black until 64 kt and above), 3D Track Altitude, S.I. Units, Simple Icon, and Real Scale (3D).
  • Measure (map header) draws a polygon, circle, or rectangle for distance and area. Mark Point (bottom bar) drops a marker, and clicking a marker opens point-data analysis you can download.

4. MMR video sync

Load a cockpit or radar .mp4 in Upload MMR (top card). When no video is loaded the tracker takes the full width.

  • Auto-Sync (default): OCR reads the timestamp burned into the video frame and aligns automatically. A green pulse means OCR is active, and a "Syncing" pill shows while it hunts. Click Sync Now to force a lock on a clear frame.
  • Manual Time Input: type the video's UTC start time in MMR Start (HHMMSS).

5. Satellite overlays

Open the Satellite picker in the map header, choose a satellite, then its product appears right below to pick. Options auto-populate from the flight's date and location.

  • MODIS / VIIRS (polar, NASA GIBS): any date back to each mission's start. A day-stepper moves between days, and overpass times are looked up automatically.
  • GOES-East / GOES-West (archive, needs API): rendered server-side from NOAA's S3 archive for the flight's historical date, refreshed on a 10-minute interval. Products (spectral bands plus Sandwich and GeoColor composites) are auto-discovered from the API and never hardcoded, so a product the API cannot currently serve shows as unavailable rather than disappearing, and only that product greys out while the satellite stays usable. A GOES option greys out entirely when the flight is outside that satellite's Earth-disk view.
  • Pre-Cache Satellite Imagery (top card) pre-downloads imagery for multiple flights at once, and the cache lasts until the tab closes.

6. Storm best-track overlay

Archive loads automatically draw the storm's whole-life, intensity-colored, dashed best-track on both trackers. Toggle it with the Storm Track checkbox, and the Last Storm Observation card shows the nearest fix.

7. Charts, PFD, and HUD

  • Eight synced charts (temperature, nav angles, flow angles, altitude, speeds, vertical speeds and accel, pressure, thermodynamics) track the playback moment. Each chart resets its own zoom and adds or removes series from its controls.
  • Create Your Own Graph plots any variables together for custom comparisons.
  • Cockpit PFD (filters): a G1000-style attitude ladder, airspeed, altitude, and heading tapes, a VSI, a bank scale with a slip/skid indicator, a wind box, and a ground-track pointer.
  • The on-map HUD shows live flight-level values at the playback moment.

8. Exporting and sharing

  • Record Clip: pick a start and end range (previewed live at both ends, so you see the exact frame without leaving the dialog), a tracker mode, a satellite overlay, an optional MMR video, and up to four graphs. The tool auto-plays the range and screen-records it to a video file.
  • Share Link: copies a URL that reopens this mission at the current playback moment and tracker view.

9. Troubleshooting

  • Archive greyed out with "API Offline": use manual upload, and it auto-recovers when the API returns.
  • A GOES option is greyed out: the API is offline, or the flight is outside that satellite's view, so try the other GOES or a MODIS/VIIRS pass.
  • Picked GOES but nothing shows: pick a product from the picker first, since products do not auto-select.
  • Auto-Sync lands on the wrong time: click Sync Now on a clear frame, hide other on-screen timestamps, or switch to Manual.
  • Sluggish with satellite on: let the pre-cache finish, or pre-cache ahead of time.
